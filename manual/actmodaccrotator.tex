Accelerated rotation, with Euler angle around z axis $\Omega_z$ as
\begin{equation}
  \Omega_z = \theta_{acc\_onset} + t \omega + \left\{\begin{array}{ll} \frac12 t^2 a & t > 0\\0 & t \le 0
  \end{array}\right.
\end{equation}
with $t$ being the time since $t_\textrm{acc\_onset}$.
