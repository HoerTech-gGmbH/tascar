Control position from an OSC stream

The translation is assumed to be in meters, the rotation is ZYX-Euler
angles in radians.

\begin{tscattributes}
  \indattr{path}          & OSC path                                                                             \\
  \indattr{inputchannels} & Number of channels in OSC variable                                                   \\
  \indattr{channels}      & LSL channels, for the six translation channels (x,y,z,rotz,roty,rotx), -1 for unused \\
  \indattr{influence}     & Weights of channels                                                                  \\
  \indattr{local}         & Use local (true) or global translation                                               \\
  \indattr{incremental}   & Use incremental changes                                                              \\
\end{tscattributes}
