Add a (band limited) frequency-dependent noise to the input
signal. The power spectral density $P$ is
\begin{equation}
P(f) \propto \frac{1}{f^\alpha}
\end{equation}
in the interval $f_{min} \le f \le f_{max}$, and zero otherwise.
